This assignment was a top ten list that was meant to be persuasive for the audience of your choosing which I completed for IDS 129.
Take Care- Drake
Started the rise of Drake to the top of Hip-hop. Drake has gone on a legendary run in the 2010s
The Carter 4- Lil Wayne
Arguably one of the best Lil Wayne albums. Created new age sound with the production on this album
Good kid, M.A.A.D. City- Kendrick Lamar
Launched Kendrick Lamar into the mainstream. Started his legendary run in the 2010s making him one of the greatest of all time.
2014 Forest Hills Drive- J.Cole
After this album, J.Cole was seen as one of the best lyricists/rappers of our generation.
DS2- Future Arguably the pinnacle of the Atlanta trap sound. Eventually this style of trap music became mainstream and this album is where Future perfected it.
What a Time to Be Alive- Drake and Future
Two hip hop juggernauts drop a collaboration album it is bound to be legendary. Their styles of rap fit perfectly with each other and they both played a major role in bringing rap into the mainstream.
Astroworld- Travis Scott
The long-awaited and massively anticipated album did not let any Travis Scott fans down.
Culture- Migos
Their wild and arrogant personalities in their music is enjoyable for some to listen to and they have an upbeat feel to it.
